Negative Values Explained
Negative daily values don't mean immediate failure! When you start a town, you accumulate resources before repairs become significant. The calculator shows hourly equilibrium - what happens after you've used up your initial stockpile. If your town is working fine despite negative values, you have accumulated resources acting as a buffer.
